## 🚨 Troubleshooting

### Site Not Indexed After 7 Days?

1. Check robots.txt isn't blocking: `https://vapewissen.de/robots.txt`
2. Verify sitemap submitted in Search Console
3. Manually request indexing again
4. Check for "Coverage" errors in Search Console

### No Traffic After 2 Weeks?

- **Normal!** SEO takes 2-3 months
- Focus on:
  - More content (10+ articles)
  - Reddit/Pinterest for immediate traffic
  - Backlinks (forum posts, comments)

### Affiliate Links Not Working?

- Verify Amazon Associates account approved
- Check link format: `https://www.amazon.de/dp/PRODUCT_ID?tag=YOUR_TAG`
- Test in incognito window
- Amazon requires 3 sales within 180 days to stay active
